About


My intent behind the website was to create a place where reliable historical information is available. I have always been interested in history, especially about how so many people in, relatively, recent history have had a massive impact on our society and even our way of thinking, and yet many people don't awknowledge them. I also wanted to show people how history can be very important to our modern life by showing how many simple decisions made by a few individuals can change our world. Right now I managed to get it to work on all platforms, but I had to sacrifice the ability to make it completely fluid.
